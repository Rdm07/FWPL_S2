Rules and Instructions:
For each gameweek of UEFA Champions League, you will be asked 10 questions. Each question can be answered by using the given multiple choice options or entering a text or numeric answer (Text answer will be the name of a palyer or a team)
While entering the text answer, enter the full name of a team or player (Paris Saint-Germain not PSG) (No need to add F.C.). The answerkey will be entered using data from whoscored.com
Enter the answers using English Characters only.
Each correct answer will be given 1 pt and not pts will be deducted for incorrect answers. You can select at the most 1 answer to be a bonus answer. The bonus answer will fetch you 2 pts if correct, but get 2 pts deducted if incorrect
Each gameweek may or may not have a compulsion on submitting a bonus answer. Check the top of the gameweek page to verify. However if for bonus-compulsory gameweek, you fail to submit a bonus answer, 2 pts will be deducted for the week.
